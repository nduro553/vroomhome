About VroomHome Cabinetry

VroomHome has been serving the community with exceptional cabinetry services since 2008. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in home improvement. We believe that every space deserves thoughtful design and meticulous execution.

Our team of skilled craftspeople combines traditional woodworking techniques with modern design principles. We source only the finest materials and employ cutting-edge installation methods to ensure your cabinets stand the test of time.

Whether you're renovating a single room or undertaking a complete home transformation, VroomHome brings expertise, professionalism, and attention to detail to every project.

Our Journey

2008 - The Beginning
VroomHome Cabinetry was founded by master craftsman Marcus Thorne with a vision to revolutionize residential cabinetry. Starting with a small workshop and a team of three, we focused on delivering exceptional quality to every client. Our first year saw us complete over 40 residential projects, establishing our reputation for reliability and craftsmanship.
2012 - Expansion & Recognition
By 2012, VroomHome had grown to a team of 15 skilled professionals. We expanded our workshop and invested in state-of-the-art equipment. That year, we received our first industry award for innovative cabinet design. Our portfolio had grown to include over 300 completed projects across residential and commercial spaces.
2016 - Commercial Division Launch
Recognizing the demand for commercial cabinetry solutions, we launched our commercial division. This expansion allowed us to serve restaurants, offices, retail spaces, and hospitality venues. Our commercial projects brought new challenges and opportunities, further refining our expertise and capabilities.
2020 - Digital Transformation
During 2020, we embraced digital innovation, implementing 3D design software and virtual consultation capabilities. This allowed us to serve clients more effectively while maintaining our commitment to personalized service. We also enhanced our sustainability practices, sourcing eco-friendly materials and reducing waste.
2024 - Present Day Excellence
Today, VroomHome operates with a team of 40+ professionals and has completed over 1,200 projects. We continue to innovate in design and installation techniques while maintaining the personal touch that defines our brand. Our commitment to excellence remains unwavering as we look toward future growth and continued service to our valued clients.

Kitchen Cabinet Trends & Innovations

The world of kitchen cabinetry is constantly evolving with new trends and innovations. Modern kitchens are embracing open shelving concepts combined with closed storage, creating visual interest while maintaining functionality. Two-tone cabinetry, where upper and lower cabinets feature different colors or finishes, continues to gain popularity. Soft-close hinges and drawer mechanisms have become standard expectations rather than luxury additions.

Smart storage solutions are revolutionizing kitchen organization. Pull-out pantries, corner carousels, and vertical dividers maximize space utilization. Integrated appliances create seamless kitchen designs, while waterfall countertops flowing from islands add contemporary elegance. Sustainable materials like reclaimed wood and bamboo are increasingly popular among environmentally conscious homeowners.

At VroomHome, we stay ahead of these trends while respecting timeless design principles. We help clients choose innovations that enhance their lifestyle without compromising durability or aesthetics. Click here to continue exploring current kitchen design trends with our team.

Bathroom Vanity Design Excellence

Bathroom vanities serve as both functional storage and design focal points. Modern bathroom design emphasizes clean lines, minimalist aesthetics, and efficient storage. Floating vanities create an illusion of space and simplify cleaning. Double vanities in master bathrooms provide convenience and luxury for shared spaces.

Material selection for bathroom vanities requires special consideration due to moisture exposure. We utilize water-resistant finishes and durable hardware specifically designed for bathroom environments. Integrated lighting, mirrors, and countertops create cohesive designs that elevate the entire bathroom experience.

Our bathroom vanity projects range from simple updates to complete renovations. We consider plumbing requirements, ventilation, and lighting to create functional, beautiful spaces. Each project reflects the homeowner's personal style while maximizing practical utility.

Maintenance & Care for Your Cabinetry

Proper maintenance ensures your cabinetry remains beautiful and functional for decades. Regular cleaning with appropriate products prevents buildup and maintains finishes. We provide detailed care instructions for each project, tailored to your specific materials and finishes.

Wood cabinets benefit from occasional conditioning to maintain luster and protect against moisture. Hardware should be checked periodically to ensure proper function. Soft-close mechanisms may require occasional adjustment as they age. We offer maintenance services and can address any issues that arise.

Understanding your cabinetry's specific needs helps extend its lifespan. Different wood species, finishes, and hardware types require different care approaches. Our team is always available to answer questions about maintaining your investment.
